Research Capabilities

Our Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) research capabilities span the full spectrum of renal medicine.

CKD Research Focus Areas

Comprehensive capabilities across renal medicine.

CKD Epidemiology

Characterizing the burden, risk factors, and progression of chronic kidney disease across diverse African populations.

Pharmacotherapy Trials

Investigating novel pharmacological interventions for CKD management, including SGLT2 inhibitors and endothelin receptor antagonists.

Dialysis Outcomes

Studying outcomes and access optimization for hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis modalities in resource-varied settings.

Biomarker Discovery

Identifying novel biomarkers for early CKD detection and progression monitoring in African patient cohorts.

Transplant Research

Advancing renal transplant protocols, immunosuppression strategies, and long-term graft survival across the continent.

Cardiorenal Syndrome

Exploring the intersection of cardiovascular and renal disease, a critical area of unmet need in African populations.

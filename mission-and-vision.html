About ARRI

The Africa Renal Research Network is a consortium of Africa's leading nephrologists, committed to bridging the gap between global pharmaceutical innovation and African clinical expertise.

Our Foundation

Built on integrity, driven by purpose.

Mission

To advance renal research across Africa by creating a high-integrity, sponsor-ready clinical trial network that produces world-class data.

Vision

A continent where every patient has access to cutting-edge kidney disease treatments through robust, locally-driven clinical research.

Integrity

GCP-certified processes, ethical oversight, and transparent data management underpin every study we conduct.

Pan-African Reach

Our network spans multiple African countries, providing access to diverse patient populations and varied clinical environments.

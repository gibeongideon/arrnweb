Mission & Vision

Vision

To be a leading centre of excellence in kidney research and care, transforming the lives of African Patients through innovative clinical trials, knowledge translation and community engagement

Start with the patient – understand their needs and deliver accurate results.

Mission

To establish a collaborative network dedicated to Advancing the understanding, prevention and treatment of kidney disease in Africa by conducting rigorous clinical trials, fostering partnerships and empowering Healthcare providers and communities to improve patient outcomes.

  • Focus on Africa: Acknowledge the unique needs and challenges of the African Population
  • Research: Emphasize the dual commitment of understanding the disease and improving patient outcomes
  • Innovation & Collaboration: Partnerships to bring our patients the best of cutting edge researchers
  • Data integrity and patient safety: Focus on transparency & accountability through rigorous adherence to protocols, training and commitment to the principles of good clinical practice

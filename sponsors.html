Typical sponsor timeline windows

Efficient Study Start-Up

ARRN coordinates confidentiality, feasibility, approvals, contracts, budgets, and activation through one structured sponsor workflow.

01

2 to 3 days

CDA

Confidentiality agreements are completed first so sponsor and site teams can begin document exchange and aligned planning.

02

2 to 4 days

Feasibility Questionnaire

Site capabilities, patient access, investigator availability, and operational readiness are documented early for sponsor review.

03

1 to 2 weeks

Regulatory Submission & Approval

Regulatory and ethics documentation is prepared and submitted for review as quickly as the local pathway allows.

04

2 to 3 weeks

Budget & Contract Execution

Budget review, contract negotiation, and execution move in parallel with approvals to avoid preventable delays.

05

3 months

Study Activation

ARRN moves from approved site setup to activation with sponsor communication, readiness checks, and site launch coordination.

Parallel workstreams
